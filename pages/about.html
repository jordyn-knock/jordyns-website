Internships

My journey has been shaped by impactful internships at LuxBio, and MOLLI Surgical, now a part of Stryker. I am currently working at LuxBio, a biotechnology company that makes light from enzymes! I work as a Bioprocess Technician where I assist in automating fermentations and the downstream processing of protein. Last summer as well as the previous summer, I worked for MOLLI Surgical during the time that they were acquired by Stryker. I developed a range of skills in product testing, failure analysis, and prototyping. I led the investigation into a sensor error, conducted non-destructive testing, and contributed to the MOLLI Accuracy Robot project.

UBC Projects

At UBC, I am an active contributor to the UBC Rapid design team, where I work on updating the website as well as working with a team on developing a filament recycler. Specifically, I work closely with two others on the shaft of the filament recycler, doing material research and necessary calculations. Here I get the experience of working with other teams to create a larger project. I completed two projects in my second year through my UBC courses. The first was researching and developing a method to produce hydrocortisone, and the second was a process to produce Oxalic Acid. There is more information about both of these on the projects page. Finally, I have completed two hackathons so far at UBC, both which also can be read about on the projects page.
