Oxalic Acid Production Plant (Sept. 2024 - Dec. 2024)

Through the UBC courses CHBE 220 and CHBE 201, myself and 3 others developed a production plant for producing oxalic acid. After comparing 3 different reaction pathways, we decided on one and created a process flow diagram (shown below), analaysing the economics of the process, separation strategies, and energy balances.

Process Flow Diagram for reactor, crystallizer and centrifuge in the oxalic acid process.

About the Project

Oxalic acid is an essential chemical for metal refinement and treatment, driving clean energy technologies. Guided through 7 tutorials, my teammates and I reaserched and proposed an oxalic acid production process for a plant in Shandong, China.

First, we wrote an expression of intrest to a client. We then compared 3 different reaction pathways, deciding on the oxidation of carbohydrates. Next, we reaserched separation strategies for our process. Following that, we analyzed energy balances, calculating the temperatures and states our reactants are in, and how much energy it will take to get them to the desired ones. We then created the above process flow diagram with a stream table. Finally, we analyzed control stategies before creating a final presentation, which you can view below.

Technical Skills
