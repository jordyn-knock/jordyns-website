HALO Space Habitat (Jan. 2023 - Apr. 2023)

The Lunar Gateway Habitation and Logistics Outpost, or more informally known as HALO, was a project myself and my 3 friends built for the Professional Engineers of Ontario Engineering Idol Competition, earning us 1st place overall.

One side of our design

The other side of our design

About the Project

This project focused on creating an efficient, comfortable, and safe living space for astronauts on the moon, while enabling long-term human habitation and supporting future deep-space missions. We tackled challenges such as radiation, extreme temperatures, and limited space, while prioritizing astronaut comfort, mental health, and structural durability.

Our solution incorporated cutting-edge materials, an optimized design for space efficiency, and innovative interior layouts. Using a square-based dome structure for strength and space maximization, and materials like aluminum alloys and ultra-high molecular weight polyethylene, we addressed environmental challenges and created a sustainable design.
